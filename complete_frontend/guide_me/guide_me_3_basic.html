  • Robot Arm Basics

    In the ongoing theme, the robot arm has two functions; to scan for fruits and to pluck and drop fruits. Most basic robot arm with 2 degrees of freedom will be least required to do the job.
    (Degrees of Freedom is an independent joint that can provide freedom of movement for the manipulator, either in a rotational or translational (linear) sense. Every geometric axis that a joint can rotate around or extend along is counted as a Single Degree of Freedom.)

    Every Robot Arm has three basic parts:
    1. Base
    2. Joints
    3. Links

    At the very last link an end effector is attached to give the robot a use case or some functionality. This end effector can be anything from a gripper, a welding torch, a paint gun, etc.
